Kota King

Kota King is South Africa's premier traditional hut construction company, specializing in authentic African kota design and construction. With over 5 years of experience, we combine ancestral building wisdom with modern engineering excellence.

Our mission is to preserve and promote African architectural heritage while providing comfortable, durable, and beautiful living spaces that honor tradition while meeting contemporary needs.

Each kota we build is a masterpiece of craftsmanship, using locally sourced materials and traditional techniques passed down through generations. Our team of skilled artisans takes pride in every detail, from the thatched roofing to the intricate wall patterns.

We serve clients across Southern Africa, including private homeowners, tourism resorts, cultural centers, and educational institutions seeking authentic African architectural experiences.
